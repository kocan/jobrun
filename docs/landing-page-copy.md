## Feature Sections

### Estimates

**Headline:** Win more work with professional estimates

Send polished estimates in seconds. Pull services from your price book, add line items, and share a link your customer can view and accept on any device. Track every estimate from draft to accepted so nothing slips through the cracks.

### Jobs & Scheduling

**Headline:** Schedule jobs and knock them out

See your day at a glance. Each job tracks the customer, location, line items, and status — scheduled, in-progress, or completed. Attach before-and-after photos and log communications so you have a full history for every property.

### Invoices

**Headline:** Invoice on the spot, get paid faster

Turn a completed job into an invoice with one tap. Invoices auto-number, calculate tax, and can be shared via link. Track status from sent to viewed to paid without chasing paper.

### Payments

**Headline:** Accept payments however your customers pay

Record cash and check payments on the spot or let customers pay online with Stripe. Every payment is tied to an invoice so your books stay clean.

### Offline Mode

**Headline:** No signal? No problem.

JobRun stores everything on your device first. Create estimates, update jobs, and record payments without cell service. Changes sync automatically the moment you're back online.

## 3-Minute Video Walkthrough Script

### Opening (0:00–0:15)

*Visual: Phone screen showing the JobRun home dashboard.*

**Voiceover:** "You started your business to do great work — not to wrestle with paperwork. JobRun handles the business side so you can stay in the field. Let me show you how it works."

### Onboarding (0:15–0:40)

*Visual: Sign-up screen, then business setup with trade selection and price book.*

**Voiceover:** "Sign up in under a minute. Pick your trade — pressure washing, detailing, lawn care, cleaning, or handyman — and JobRun loads a price book with services and pricing you can customize. Add your business name, and you're ready to go."

### Creating an Estimate (0:40–1:10)

*Visual: Tapping New Estimate, selecting a customer, adding line items from the price book, previewing, and sharing.*

**Voiceover:** "A customer calls for a quote. Tap New Estimate, pick the customer — or add them right here — then pull services from your price book. Adjust quantities, add notes, and hit Share. Your customer gets a clean, professional estimate they can accept from any browser."

### Scheduling a Job (1:10–1:40)

*Visual: Estimate status changing to Accepted, then creating a job from it. Job detail screen with date, time, and line items.*

**Voiceover:** "When they accept, create a job from the estimate with one tap. Set the date and time. On job day, mark it in-progress, snap before-and-after photos, and mark it complete when you're done."

### Invoicing (1:40–2:10)

*Visual: Tapping 'Create Invoice' from the completed job. Invoice preview with line items and tax. Sharing the invoice link.*

**Voiceover:** "From the completed job, tap Create Invoice. Line items, tax, and totals carry over automatically. Share the invoice link and your customer can view it instantly. You'll see when it's been viewed so you never have to wonder."

### Recording Payment (2:10–2:35)

*Visual: Invoice status showing 'Viewed'. Recording a payment — selecting Stripe, cash, or check. Status updating to Paid.*

**Voiceover:** "When they pay, record it in seconds. Cash, check, or Stripe — pick the method, confirm the amount, and the invoice is marked paid. Your records are always up to date."

### Offline Mode (2:35–2:50)

*Visual: Airplane mode toggled on. User creates an estimate and updates a job. Airplane mode off, sync indicator spinning, data appearing.*

**Voiceover:** "Working somewhere without signal? JobRun keeps going. Create estimates, update jobs, record payments — all offline. When you're back online, everything syncs automatically."

### Closing (2:50–3:00)

*Visual: Return to home dashboard showing today's schedule, recent invoices, and a payment notification.*

**Voiceover:** "Book it. Do it. Get paid. That's JobRun. Download it free today."
